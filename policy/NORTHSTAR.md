## The frame

Two halves of the AI gold rush.

**The other half** (what most are building):
AI to replace people for revenue. Sales replacement, marketing replacement,
data dashboards that still need a human to act. The market still decides
if any of it works.

**Our half**:
AI to govern operational execution. Cuts decision cycles from quarters to
days. Cuts build cycles from sprints to hours. Reduces meeting time and
closes cross-functional cracks. Augments the workforce, never replaces them.

> **We build the half it all runs on.**

## What we build for

**Operators** — the COOs, VPs of Ops, founders, fractional leaders who:
- Have run the rooms they're now buying tools for
- Are tired of consultants who can't operate
- Need outcomes, not engagement-quality dashboards
- Know that strategy fails in the handoff, not in the slide

**Not for**: pure VPs of sales, growth marketers, or anyone whose job
ends at the lead gen stage. Those needs are better served by the other
half of the gold rush.
